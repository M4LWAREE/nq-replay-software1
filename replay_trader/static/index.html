Trades

How to use: Press Play (or Space) to start the tape. Bars build tick-by-tick in real time. BUY/SELL (keys B/S) open a market order at the next print +slip; set Stop/Target first or edit them live then Apply. FLATTEN (F) closes now. The date is hidden and prices are offset — you're reading the tape blind. Your net is scored against a coin-flip taking the same trades in a random direction: edge/trade is your real edge. End & Reveal shows the date.
